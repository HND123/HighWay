      Rules & Regulations

      Before starting the journey


         
      • Plan and decide the entry and exit points.
        •  
      • Check whether the vehicle has enough fuel for the whole journey in the Expressway
        •  
      • Make sure that you have enough money required to pay “user fee”.
        •  
      • Always read instructions/ information displayed in direction signs before proceeding to entry ramp.

      Before starting the journey


         
      • Vehicles exiting the interchange should be in the outer lane when passing the 500 m ahead sign of the exit point, switch on ”turning to left” signal light. Speed reducing lane, begins in a short while. Drive towards the exit point reducing the speed.
        •  
      • You should never make a sudden change of lanes from inner lane to exit ramp, by cutting across the outer lane, when you see the exit sign. Such movement could lead to serious accidents involving several vehicles.
        •  
      • If you miss the exit ramp, you should not reverse or turn back and drive against traffic. You should drive up to the nearest interchange and come back.
